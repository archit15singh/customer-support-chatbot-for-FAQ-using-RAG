How to change email address on X | X Help Skip to main content Help Center How to update your email address Having an up-to- date email address attached to your account is a great step toward improved account security.Note: Each time the email address associated with your X account is updated, we will send an email notification to the previously-used email address alerting you of this change.For more information on these types of alerts, read about account security. In addition, we will continue to store your previously-used email addresses, and will use this information for safety and security purposes.You can access a complete history of the email addresses associated with your account by downloading your data through Your X Data.Update your email address Step 1 Tap the navigation menu icon, then tap Settings and privacy.Step 2 Tap Account.Step 3 Tap Email.Step 4 Insert your email address and tap Done. Note : An email address can only be associated with one X account at a time.Step 1 In the top menu, you will either see a navigation menu icon or your profile icon.Tap whichever icon you have, and select Settings and privacy.Step 2 Tap Account.Step 3 Tap Email.Step 4 Insert your email address and tap Next.Note : An email address can only be associated with one X account at a time.Step 1 Log in to twitter.com and go to your Account settings page by clicking the more icon and then Settings and privacy.Step 2 Click Your account.Step 3 Click Account information and enter your password.Step 4 Click Email.Step 5 Type your email address into the Email field. Note : An email address can only be associated with one X account at a time.Step 6 Click the Save button at the bottom of the page.Note: Your email address is not displayed in your public profile on X.If you have not turned off the Let others find me by my email address setting, then others that already have your email address may find your X account.Learn more about your email and phone number discoverability privacy settings.
