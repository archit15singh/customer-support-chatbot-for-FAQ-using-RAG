How to post links in a Post Skip to main content Help Center How to post links in a Post All links (URLs) posted in posts are shortened using our t.co service.When viewing the post on x.com the original URL (or shortened version of the original URL) will be displayed.Below are instructions on how to post a link in a post on the web and on your mobile device.How to post a link in a Post Step 1 Open your preferred mobile app, or begin a new text message.Step 2 Type or paste the URL into your Post. Links will be adjusted with X's link shortener wherever you post them.Some clients will adjust your character count as you compose a Post; some won't.Step 3 Post.Step 1 Open your preferred mobile app, or begin a new text message.Step 2 Type or paste the URL into your Post.
