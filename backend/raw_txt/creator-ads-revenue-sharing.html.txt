Creator Ads Revenue Sharing | X Help Skip to main content Help Center Ads Revenue Sharing Ads revenue sharing lets you share revenue from verified user's organic impressions of ads displayed in replies to content you post on X.This is part of our effort to help people earn a living directly on X.Creators will be able to set up Ads Revenue Sharing and Creator Subscriptions independently.Ads Revenue Sharing will be available globally to creators who meet our eligibility requirements.See below for full availability.How you can become eligible To be considered eligible for creator ads revenue sharing you must: Be subscribed to X Premium or Verified Organizations.Have at least 5M organic impressions on your cumulative posts within the last 3 months.Have at least 500 followers What youâll need once you are eligible A Stripe account.We work with Stripe, our payment processor, for payouts.Adhere to our Ads Revenue Share Terms , which include, for example, Creator Monetization Standards and the X Rules. Where to access Ads Revenue Sharing We want the process to be as simple as possible, so all eligible X Premium and Verified Organizations subscribers are entitled to revenue share so long as they meet the eligibility criteria and join. Users will be able to apply for both Creator Subscriptions and Ads Revenue Sharing by accessing Monetization in settings here. Note, while all eligible users may choose to participate, if you are found to breach the Ads Revenue Share Terms , which include, for example, Creator Monetization Standards and the X Rules , you may be excluded from the program. How it works Eligible users will be able to join and set up payments from within the Monetization section of the app.This is found in the side menu on iOS and Android, and the overflow menu on web.Once you click âJoin and setup payoutsâ you will be redirected to our payment processor, Stripe, to setup an account to receive your share.This Stripe account will be where you will be able to transfer funds to your external bank account. Once you opt in, you will receive payouts at a regular cadence, so long as you have generated more than $10 USD.X may modify or cancel the Program at any time in its sole discretion, including for business, financial, or legal reasons.X reserves the right to accept or revoke your participation in this ads revenue share program in its sole discretion, including for business, financial, or legal reasons.Please ensure you comply with the Ads Revenue Program Terms.How to deactivate To withdraw from the ads revenue sharing program, you can contact X Paid Support to notify us that youâd like to stop receiving payouts. How to appeal if your account is flagged for review If you receive notification that your payouts have been paused because your account has been flagged for review you will have an opportunity to appeal. Your account will continue accrue revenue until a final decision is made.This includes accounts that have been suspended. All participants will have the opportunity to appeal if their account is flagged. You will have 30 days to appeal by writing to X by responding to the email we will send to you with meaningful evidence that your program should be restarted.If you do not appeal within 30 days or we reject your appeal, you will be made ineligible to restart the Program.How to appeal if your account is paused at any point during the program If you receive notification that your payouts have been paused because your account has been found to be in breach of our terms and/or policies after receiving payment you will have an opportunity to appeal.Your account will continue accrue revenue until a final decision is made.This includes accounts that have been suspended.
