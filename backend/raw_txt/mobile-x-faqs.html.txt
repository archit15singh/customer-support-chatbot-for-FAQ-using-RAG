mobile.X.com FAQs Skip to main content Help Center mobile.X.com FAQs How do I post a reply? Locate the post you'd like to reply to.Tap the reply icon on the post you wish to reply to.A compose box will pop up, where you will see the name of the person(s) you are directly replying to.Type your reply into the post box as prompted.Tap post to send your message.How do I send a Direct Message on mobile.X.com? Tap the message icon and then tap the compose icon Start typing the username of the person you want to send a message to and select from the list of accounts that display.Compose your message and when you're ready to send it, tap Send. Read more about Direct Messages.How do I accept follow requests on mobile.X.com? Tap your profile icon.Tap Follower requests.To accept the request, tap the checkmark.To decline the request, tap the X.How do I block an account?
